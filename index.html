About the Club

Our mission, vision, and values

Student-led and purpose-driven, the club uses pickleball to grow leadership, enterprise, wellness, and belonging across the MUBAS community.

Club Mission

Empowering Student Through Sport, Entrepreneurship, Mental Health, and Faith-Driven Values

About Us

The MUBAS Pickleball Club is a dynamic, purpose-driven sporting community rooted at the Malawi University of Business and Applied Sciences. More than just a sports club, we are a movement committed to championing Pickleball as a catalyst for innovation, entrepreneurship, mental wellness, and holistic student development. Through Pickleball, we create a platform where sport meets enterprise, fostering leadership, creativity, resilience, and community engagement. The club serves as a living laboratory for applied learning where students translate classroom knowledge into real-world impact through sport-based initiatives, events, and ventures.

vision

Profile Presentation

To become a leading university-based Pickleball hub in Africa, shaping well-rounded individuals by integrating sport, entrepreneurship, mental health, and wellness into a powerful culture of excellence, inclusion, and innovation.

What We Stand For

Profile Presentation

Pickleball for Growth & Community

We use pickleball not only as a fun and inclusive sport but as a way to build teamwork, discipline, and resilience among young people. The club actively hosts training sessions, tournaments, and outreach programs to expand the game across Malawi.

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

We equip our members with business skills, financial literacy, and creative problem-solving abilities, ensuring they can turn their ideas into sustainable ventures. Our club organizes workshops, networking events, and mentorship programs to nurture future business leaders.

Mental Health & Wellness

Recognizing the challenges students and youth face, we promote mental health awareness, peer support groups, and stress-relief activities to ensure our members grow in confidence and emotional resilience.

Faith & Values

Guided by the principles of love, integrity, and service, we believe in mentorship and spiritual nourishment as key components of personal and community transformation. We hold fellowship gatherings, prayer sessions, and discussions on applying faith in everyday life.
